longDescription: |
  Planet Builder (shipped as TerraForge) is a browser toy for growing worlds. You
  tune elemental composition and orbit, the simulation compresses five billion years
  of planetary evolution into about six seconds as an animated point-cloud, and the
  result is saved and shareable by link. A community gallery shows recent worlds
  others have forged. It is lightweight, instantly gratifying, and a great demo of
  procedural simulation landed in a friendly UI.
